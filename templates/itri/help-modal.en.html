Help

  • Use * to match zero or more words, e.g., play * role  ->  play a role and play an important role
  • Use _ to match any word, e.g., listen _ music  ->  listen to music
  • Use ~WORD to search for the similar words of a WORD, e.g., ~reliable person  ->  trustworthy person
  • Use ?WORD to search for WORD optionallyï¼Œ e.g., listen ?to music  ->  listen to music
  • Use WORD1/WORD2 (slash) to choose either WORD1 or WORD2 e.g., go in/to school  ->  go to school
  • Use POS (Part of Speech) to search for word with specific pos tag, e.g., v. a report  ->  submit a report
    POS can be: v. (verb) n. (noun) adj. (adjective) prep. (preposition) det. (determiner) conj. (conjunction) pron. (pronoun)
    Remember to keep a dot(.) after each pos tag
  • Note: the maximum number of words in a query is 5
